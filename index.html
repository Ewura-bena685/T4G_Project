  • ✨ Prioritize rest and sleep
  • 🍲 Eat iron-rich foods (leafy greens, red meat)
  • 💧 Stay hydrated
  • 🧘 Gentle movement like yoga or walking
  • 🛁 Warm baths or heating pads for comfort
